WATER: Venus has very tiny amounts of water in its atmosphere, but definitely not enough for us!

ATMOSPHERE: The atmosphere is really thick and really toxic, so stay away! It's mostly made of carbon dioxide (96%) and some nitrogen (3%).

GRAVITY: Venus has a similar gravity to Earth's. You'd weigh a bit lighter, though!

welcome to venus!

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and the brightest after the Moon! It's the second biggest rocky planet and is really similar to Earth in size. Venus is also the hottest planet in our solar system, with an average temperature of 462 degrees Celsius. Now that's hot!
