home, sweet home!

One of the only planets with the perfect ingredients for life, Earth is completely unique in the solar system. Let's find out just how lucky we are to be here! Can you find the things needed for life to be here?

Did you know that you can survive without food for more than three weeks, but only three days without water? Our bodies are made of 60% water after all. Drink lots every day!

Oxygen is produced by plants, and it's what we mean when we say "the air we breathe." Take a deep breath! This is really hard to find on other planets.

You need a solid surface on a planet to have soil. Soil is also where we get all of our food from, and you need to eat to survive, after all!

An atmosphere is what keeps us from burning under the sun's rays! It also traps in oxygen so we can breathe. Otherwise, all that oxygen is just going to drift off into space!

How are we not drifting off into space?! Gravity is what keeps us on the ground.

Have you been through a really hot summer, or a really cold winter? The reason our summers aren't so hot that we actually burn to death is our very own moon! The moon is our weather controller.
