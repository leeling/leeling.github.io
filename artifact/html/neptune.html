WATER: There's a bit of frozen water in Neptune's atmosphere, but nothing drinkable for us!

ATMOSPHERE: The atmosphere on Neptune is mostly made of hydrogen and helium, with some methane.

GRAVITY: The gravitational pull here is almost the same as Earth's. You would feel a little heavier, though!

MOON: Neptune has 14 moons! This is actually the least number of moons compared to other gas giants.

Neptune is the eighth planet from the Sun, though it might actually have first formed way closer! Because it spins so quickly, it has some of the fastest winds in the solar system. Neptune is the smallest of the gas giants.
