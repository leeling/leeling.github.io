SOIL: Mars technically does have soil! It's mostly made of dust, and has sodium, potassium, chloride and magnesium.

ATMOSPHERE: There's a very thing atmosphere, mostly made of carbon dioxide (what we breathe out!).

GRAVITY: Surface gravity is only 37% of Earth's, which means you weigh almost three times less on Mars!

MOON: Mars has two small moons; their names in Latin mean "Fear" and "Panic"!

welcome to the red planet!

Mars is the fourth planet from the moon, and has the largest dust storms in the solar system. It's considered a rocky planet, which means it's mostly made of rock, like Earth!
