ATMOSPHERE: The atmosphere here is mostly made of hydrogen and helium, with some methane gas (not good for breathing!).

GRAVITY: Gravity is a bit weaker than on Earth, so you can jump higher!

Brrr, it's cold here! Temperatures here are an average of -224 degrees Celsius, can you imagine how cold that would be? Uranus is the farthest planet from the Sun, and the first to be discovered by telescope. It's often called the "ice giant."
