WATER: Water ice might exist in the bottoms of craters at Mercury's poles (the most northern and most southern tips)!

SOIL: The soil on Mercury is really thin dust! This is almost the same as the thin "soil" on our moon.

ATMOSPHERE: There's a very thin, weak atmosphere, mostly made of dust blown around by solar winds (wind caused by the Sun!).

GRAVITY: Mercury has really weak gravity, only 38% of Earth's! This means you would be able to jump three times higher here!
